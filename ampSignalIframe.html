'Indian men's team never did this': Ashwin salutes Harmanpreet Kaur's rare gesture

R Ashwin praised Harmanpreet Kaur for a humble post-match gesture that moved Mithali Raj and Anjum Chopra after India's Women's World Cup triumph.

The Indian women's cricket team scripted history on Sunday, defeating South Africa by 52 runs in the final to clinch the ICC Women's Cricket World Cup for the first time since 2013. Captain Harmanpreet Kaur led from the front with a commanding knock that set the platform for India's total of 260/8.

After the match, in a moment that captured the hearts of cricket fans across the country, Harmanpreet was seen seeking the blessings of former captain Mithali Raj and veteran commentator Anjum Chopra, both of whom were visibly moved by the gesture.

Speaking on his YouTube channel, Ravichandran Ashwin called it a rare display of respect and humility, noting that such gestures are seldom seen in the men's dressing room.
